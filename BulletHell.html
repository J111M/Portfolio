Bullet Hell

Bullet Hell is a Top-Down shooter game where you have to evade your own bullets. Pick or shoot the coins to increase your score and try to stay alive as long as possible. The point of the game is to get a high score and then try to beat that score.

Shooting

Shooting is really important in this game it's one of the core mechanic. I made it so you shoot automatically otherwise the game would be to easy. You shoot 1 bullet every second. If you want to shoot faster you can just press the left mouse button yourself. This gives you the choice if you want to play safe or agressive to get a lot of points in a short time.

Highscore

A big part of the game is to get a good score. Thats why i added a highscore it's always fun to try and beat your own score and get the satisfaction of getting better. I made the score with playerprefs so the highscore can be used in different scenes.
