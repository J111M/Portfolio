Vaccinatie, Please

This is a project made in a team of 4 people 3 developers and 1 artist. The project is made to make people aware of the different covid symptoms. The game takes place at an airport and you have to decide who can step on the plane and who not by looking at their passport and the symptoms of the person in front of you. You can check the criteria in the bottom right corner.

My part in this project

I worked on the approve and denied buttons so you can decide who can go on the plane and who not. I made the passport able to switch between small and big so you can see what's inside. Another thing I made is the mark that indicates if a passport is real or not and the logic that decides when someone is sick or has a vaccine. And at last I assembled the code of my fellow teammates to make the game playable.

What I learned from this project

What I learned in this project is to make a serious game with a goal behind it. It was really hard to focus on the main theme of the game and not stray to far from it by adding new things. One thing that helped a lot with this was to make a plan on paper with the things you want to add and make it more specific so you don't add unnecessary things.
